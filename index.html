Our Teams

Under 10 (U10)

We have entered a U10 team into a league for this season (22/23). The format of the U10 age group is such that we can borrow and lend players to ensure everyone gets ice time, so this was a logical starting point. We have been allocated into a league with Chelmsford, Milton Keynes and Peterborough.

Under 12 (U12)

The rules in the U12 age group differ, where the requirement to enter a team is 15+1. When submitting our plans for the 2022-23 season, we did not have the numbers to commit to a U12 league team. This does not mean they will go unchallenged; WE have contacted clubs to line up games throughout the season and training will continue as usual.

Under 14 (U14)

We believe we are in a favourable position to move forward with the older age groups into next season (2023/24). We are keen to maintain interest in this age group and feather the nest for the U12 players moving up to U14 next year. We hope to get the U14/U16 players on the ice sporadically through the season to maintain a presence of our desire to expand.

Training

Training Times

Our training times for the foreseeable future have been confirmed with Cambridge Ice Arena. Initially, all players will train together as U10/U12 group. The timings will alter slightly when our membership increases and we have enough to split into separate U10 and U12 groups.

Sessions will be within the training times below.

Wednesday evening
1845 ‐ 2045

Friday evening
1715 ‐ 1915

Sunday afternoon
Skating-specific sessions will be a permanent fixture from November 2022 onwards and cost £10 per player per session.

Specific age group timings and games are posted in our Stack Team app.

Player Kit

Players must have full kit to participate in training and games.

  • Helmet
  • Neck guard
  • Shoulder pads
  • Elbow pads
  • Jersey
  • Jack or Jill
  • Padded shorts
  • Shin pads
  • Socks
  • Skates
  • Water bottle

Membership

Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly subscriptions will be set at £65 per month per player; subsequent players from the same family will be eligible for a reduced rate. This fee will enable the playing member to participate for a minimum of two hours per week of on-ice sessions.

EIHA Registration

Players must also be registered with the English Ice Hockey Association.
